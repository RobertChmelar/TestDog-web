Why TestDog?

Getting a dog is a commitment for years. TestDog lets you try the routine before you bring a real dog home.

Real time, no cheating

Real steps

Proof for the whole family

How to play

The goal is to keep your dog happy and healthy for the whole challenge. It runs in real time — your dog needs you even when the app is closed.

🍖

Feeding twice a day

Breakfast (6–10) and dinner (17–22). Miss a window and your dog gets hungry. Notifications remind you when it's time to feed.

🦮

Real walks

Morning and evening walks are mandatory. You complete one by taking 1,500 real steps — the app reads them from your phone's pedometer. An unwalked dog makes a mess and may even run away.

🧹

Cleanup

When your dog makes a mess, clean up after them. Walks stay locked until you clean up. Cleaning up lifts their mood.

🎾

Play & mood

Play and walks lift your dog's mood. When it drops to zero, they fall into depression and go on strike — only play helps then.

❤️

Lives

Serious neglect (running away, collapsing from hunger, or long depression) costs a life. When they run out, the challenge ends — but you can start again.

🏅

Stamps & certificate

Earn a stamp for every flawless, healthy day. Last the whole challenge? You get the “I can take care of a dog” certificate.

A shorter challenge is for trying it out; a longer one is greater proof of readiness — and gives you more lives.

And when you do it all without a single paid rescue, your certificate is gold. You can save and share the finished certificate.

FAQ

How are walks measured?

The app uses your phone's pedometer. You complete a walk by taking the target number of steps during the window (1,000, 1,500 or 2,000 depending on the chosen difficulty). So you really have to go outside, not just tap a button.

What happens when I close the app?

Nothing stops. The game runs in real time in the background — your dog gets hungry and still needs walking. When you reopen the app, the state catches up. Notifications remind you about feeding and walks.

What if I neglect my dog?

Serious neglect costs a life (running away, collapsing from hunger, long depression). When your lives run out, the challenge ends — but feel free to start again. Longer challenges give you more lives.

How much does it cost?

The game itself is free. You only pay for optional boosts and rescues (for example when you're running late), which you don't need at all to finish the challenge honestly. And note: using a paid rescue means your certificate won't be gold.
